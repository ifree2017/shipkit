# Workflow: Feedback

Use after repeated errors, failed gates, review comments, incidents, or retrospectives.

## Steps

1. Capture feedback.
2. Classify root cause.
3. Decide whether to update docs, templates, skills, gates, workflows, or adapters.
4. Create follow-up task.
5. Verify that future runs benefit from the update.

## Outputs

- control/feedback-log.md
- docs/10-retro/harness-improvements.md
