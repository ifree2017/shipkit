name: incident
description: Use when a production, release, or customer-facing failure affects users, data, integrations, availability, trust, or delivery acceptance.
---

# Incident

Incident handling order:
1. Contain
2. Recover
3. Communicate
4. Investigate
5. Prevent recurrence

For each incident, produce:
1. Summary
2. Timeline
3. Impact
4. Severity
5. Detection method
6. Containment
7. Recovery
8. Root cause
9. Customer communication
10. Prevention actions

Do not prioritize refactoring or blame during containment.
