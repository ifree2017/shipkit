name: risk

# Risk

Risks are future possibilities, not current blockers.

For each risk, produce:
1. Risk statement
2. Cause
3. Probability
4. Impact
5. Affected stage
6. Trigger
7. Mitigation
8. Contingency
9. Owner
10. Review date

If the risk becomes active and stops work, convert to blocker.
