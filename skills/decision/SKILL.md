name: decision
description: Use when a product, architecture, scope, release, commercial, or delivery decision has long-term project impact and must be recorded.
---

# Decision / ADR

Important decisions must preserve context, options, and consequences.

For each decision, produce:
1. Context
2. Decision needed
3. Options considered
4. Decision
5. Rationale
6. Consequences
7. Risks
8. Revisit trigger
9. Owner
10. Date

Do not only record what was chosen. Also record why alternatives were rejected.
